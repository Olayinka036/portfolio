About Me

I’m a multi-skilled creative and IT professional based in Lagos, Nigeria, with over 4 years of hands-on experience in graphic design, WordPress website development, IT support, and computer networking.

I started my journey at Ilupeju Senior Secondary School, continued with foundational computer training at the ELP program (TREM), and I'm currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Public Administration. I'm also a chartered member of the Institute of Local Government and Public Administration of Nigeria.

My passion lies in blending visual creativity with smart technology—designing everything from logos and flyers to responsive WordPress websites, while managing digital systems and networks through tools like cPanel, HTML, and CSS.

I’ve earned multiple certifications with Cisco (Cybersecurity, Networking Basics, and more), and continue to grow my skills in frontend development and digital infrastructure.

📌 Tools & Skills: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, WordPress, Elementor, HTML, and CSS, Figma, cPanel, MS Word, MS PowerPoint, MS Excel
